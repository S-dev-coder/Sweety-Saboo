About Me

Hii ! I am Sweety Saboo.

A devoted company secretary with a profound connection to spirituality and yoga.
My transformative journey began back in 2004 under the insightful guidance of Sri Sri Ravishankar ji. From that point forward, my life has been a fusion of professional responsibilities and a deep passion for enhancing both mental and physical well-being through Yoga, Meditation, and even the ancient Indian Martial Art, Kalaripayattu.

I take immense pride in being a certified yoga trainer, allowing me to share the wisdom and benefits of this ancient practice with others. In West Bengal, I hold the role of a Sri Sri Yoga coordinator, allowing me to contribute to the community's well-being and growth.

One of my most fulfilling endeavors involves leading a vibrant team of 103 young individuals in Kolkata, collectively known as the "Youth of Kolkata". Our shared mission revolves around harnessing the boundless energy of the youth to create a positive impact on society. Moreover, I'm currently dedicated to working alongside a group of resilient young girls who have been rescued, with the goal of bringing them peace, strength, and the joy they deserve.

My achievements also extend to successfully organizing and conducting the International Day of Yoga for a diverse gathering of 800 participants, transcending barriers of caste, religion, and profession. My skill set further expands to encompass India's martial art form, Kalaripayattu. I firmly believe in nurturing the youth's holistic development by not just focusing on academic knowledge, but also on ancient texts and practical skills.

My journey has been marked by reaching out to lives across different communities, teaching them the globally renowned technique of Sudarshan Kriya. I've ventured into the remotest corners of Bihar to impart basic education and meditation practices to both children and elders. Beyond my pursuits in yoga and martial arts, I find solace in playing the Veena and expressing myself through dance.

With an unwavering vision, I aspire to spread happiness and bring smiles to every face by cultivating physical and mental strength. For me, the mantra that resonates is, "A calm and joyful mind serves as the fertile ground for creativity and dynamism". Through my endeavors, I hope to contribute positively to the world around me, leaving it a better place for future generations.
